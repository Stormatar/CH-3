About Us

Wild Rescues is a registerd non profit wildlife rescue rehabilitation facility, located in Ocala Florida. We help injured, sick, neglected, and orphaned wildlife. We help rehabilitate these animals and then release them back into the wild, if possible.

Wild Rescues is a member of the National Wildlife Rehabilitators Association

We help many animals, such as

  • Racoons
  • Squirrels
  • Fox
  • Birds
  • Horses
  • Deer
  • Pigs
  • Reptiles

Did you find an injured or ophaned animal? Contact us to see if we can help.
